Stranger Things

Synopsis

Stranger Things is a science fiction-horror web television series created, written and directed by the Duffer Brothers. The show is set in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana in the 1980s and follows the mysterious disappearance of a young boy and the supernatural events that occur in the town.
